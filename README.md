# 🌍 Environmental Impact of Food Production Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
Food production is a major driver of **environmental degradation**, contributing to **climate change, water scarcity, biodiversity loss, and land overuse**. This project analyzes the **environmental footprint of different food types, farming methods, and geographic regions** to provide data-driven insights for sustainability.

---

## 🔄 CRISP-DM Framework Implementation

### **1️⃣ Business Understanding**
- **How does food production contribute to environmental issues?**
- **Which foods have the highest carbon emissions and water usage?**
- **How can policymakers and consumers make more sustainable choices?**

### **2️⃣ Data Understanding**
📂 **Dataset Information:**
- `Food_Production.csv` – Includes 43 food types, carbon emissions, water consumption, and land use.

### **3️⃣ Data Preparation**
✅ **Cleaning missing values**  
✅ **Standardizing environmental impact metrics**  
✅ **Merging related datasets for a comprehensive analysis**  

### **4️⃣ Modeling & Analysis**
🔍 **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**  
📈 **Regression Models** – Relationship between food production and CO₂ emissions  
📊 **Clustering** – Grouping foods based on sustainability factors  

### **5️⃣ Evaluation & Insights**
- **Which food products contribute most to environmental degradation?**
- **How can we reduce the ecological impact of our diets?**

### **6️⃣ Deployment & Reporting**
📡 **Interactive Dashboard** – Data visualization for policymakers  
📄 **Final Report** – Actionable recommendations for sustainability
